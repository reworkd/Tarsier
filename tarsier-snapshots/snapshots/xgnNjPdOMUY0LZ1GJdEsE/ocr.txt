    **Front - End Engineer - UI Component Library**

    GUADALAJARA, MEXICO

    C3.ai, Inc. ( NYSE: AI ) is a leading provider of Enterprise Al software for accelerating digital transformation. The proven C3
    Al Platform provides comprehensive services to build enterprise - scale Al applications more efficiently and cost - effectively
    than alternative approaches. The core of the C3 Al offering is an open, data - driven Al architecture that dramatically
    simplifies data science and application development. Learn more at: C3 AI
    We are hiring front - end engineers at all levels to lead and maintain the UI component library of the C3 Al Application
    Platform. Our UI component library allows our customers to rapidly deliver data - rich Al applications and scale them to
    handle TBS of data, and we want to take it to the next level.
    Responsibilities:
      • Lead the development of new UI components to be used by fellow engineers.
      • Maintain an ever - growing library of components to be used across different applications and industries.
      • Research and implement internal tools to increase the productivity of Engineering, UX, and PMs.
      • Provide technical guidance and support for our UX and PM teams.
    Qualifications:

      • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or related fields.
      • Experience working with client - side technologies including Javascript, Typescript, React / Redux.
       • Experience with version control systems, including Git.
      • Prior experience designing and implementing reusable UI components.
       • Experience working as part of a cross - functional team of PM, UX, and Engineering.
    Preferred Qualifications:

      • Prior experience developing a UI component library or design system for other engineers.
      • Proven experience with pixel - perfect execution of UI components.
       • Working knowledge of accessibility best practices.
       • Ability to effectively articulate complex concepts to technical and non - technical audiences.
      • Excellent analytical and troubleshooting skills.
    C3 Al provides a competitive compensation package and excellent benefits.
