                                                                                                                                                                            Y Combinator is best known for our accelerator batch program, which has helped companies
                                                                                                                                                                            like Airbnb, Stripe and Instacart get started. What is lesser known are the resources that YC
                                                                              Туре                                                                                           provides after the batch -- our YC - only hiring platform ( workatastartup.com ), regular event

                                                                              Department                                                                                     scale our impact even more.
                                                                              Software                                                                                      You'll be working directly with two former YC founders / engineers ( from Twilio, Twitter & Lyft ),
                                                                                                                                                                             and a small software team full of kind people who are eager to help you get up - to - speed. You'll
                                                                                                                                                                             be relied upon not only to help us tackle our hefty roadmap, but to also explore how YC can
                                                                                                                                                                            leverage its unique structural advantages to help our founders hire great people and build their
                                                                                                                                                                             companies after the batch ends.
                                                                                                                                                                             In this role, you will:
                                                                                                                                                                                      • Design and build out new features in entirety. Our particular team focuses on helping
                                                                                                                                                                                             founders post - batch. This is a range of features / products, with a focus on 1. )
                                                                                                                                                                                            workatastartup.com ( hiring ) and 2. ) internal software ( helping YC stay in touch with
                                                                                                                                                                                             founders and YC alumni ).
                                                                                                                                                                                      • Scope and prioritize items on our post - batch roadmap alongside YC partners and others
                                                                                                                                                                                             on the YC software team.
                                                                                                                                                                                      • Analyze usage and engagement from both founders, job - seekers and other users to
                                                                                                                                                                                             understand what's working / not working, and make things continuously better.
                                                                                                                                                                            What are we looking for
                                                                                                                                                                            YC was started by hackers, so we recognize good hackers when we see them. Much like when
                                                                                                                                                                            we read applications for startups, we're looking to work with people who generally impress us.
                                                                                                                                                                             If you've written some impressive software before, whether for yourself or a company, that's the
                                                                                                                                                                             best qualifier we have.
                                                                                                                                                                             This is a good job for someone who is excited about startups and interested in learning more
                                                                                                                                                                             about them. You've likely worked at or started a startup, or want to do so in the future.
                                                                                                                                                                             The software team has a lot of individual responsibility and access to sensitive information, so
                                                                                                                                                                            we need to hire people we can trust. Your ability to make good trade - offs and exercise good
                                                                                                                                                                            judgement is particularly important to us.
